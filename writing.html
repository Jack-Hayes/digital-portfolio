Writing Portfolio

There's something deeply satisfying about taking a dense scientific or engineering concept and finding the story buried inside--the moment when technical jargon transforms into something that actually matters to people. I write because I believe the best science happens when we can share it widely, when the boundaries between expert and curious observer start to blur.
